Floofus Fluffus Meowtus

Nocturnal and aloof, at times, the indoor Floofus Fluffus Meowtus makes you work for your keep.

  • Catterific Name: Fluffus Meowtus
  • Floof Amount: 50/11 cattimeters.
    It sheds enough fur to spawn a second Meowtus. One for the price of two.
  • Cattspan: Strong. 999+ years
